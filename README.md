# 📊 Amazon Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Amazon product sales performance using key business KPIs such as:

- YTD Sales
- QTD Sales
- YTD Products Sold
- YTD Reviews
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Month
- Sales by Week
- Top Products by Sales
- Top Products by Reviews

The goal of this project is to help business users quickly understand product performance, customer engagement, and sales trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Amazon-style e-commerce businesses need to track:

- How much revenue is generated year-to-date?
- How are quarterly sales performing?
- Which product categories contribute the most sales?
- Which products are getting the most customer attention?
- Are sales increasing or decreasing over time?

This dashboard answers these questions using interactive Power BI visuals.

## 📈 Dashboard Visuals

### 1. Sales by Month

Shows monthly sales trends and helps identify seasonal performance changes.

### 2. Sales by Week

Tracks weekly sales movement to understand short-term performance patterns.

### 3. Sales by Product Category

Compares product categories based on sales contribution.

### 4. Top Products by Sales

Identifies the products generating the highest revenue.

### 5. Top Products by Reviews

Highlights products with the highest customer engagement.
